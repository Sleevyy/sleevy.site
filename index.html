WELCOME TO MY CRIB!

My name is Emely Schadt, I am 22 years old and I currently study Animation and Game at Hochschule Darmstadt in my 6th semester.

I have been studying since fall 2021 and in summer 2025, I will hopefully be done with my bachelor thesis!

My love and passion for games started, when I was a child, playing all of the classics, mostly from nintendo. Over the years, my taste in games developed a little further into liking more and more small, passionate indie games. It all started out with Pinstripe, followed by Hollow Knight, Spiritfarer (which had me crying 80% of the time), Cult of the Lamb, Ooblets, Minute of Islands and so on! Overall, one could say I love the wonky-stylized aesthetic!

My dream is to work in the indie-game industry at some point in my life! I love the diversity, the possibilities and a small teamsize, so I can know all my co-workers names and their silly little interests, to crochet them a bunch of figures they don't really need! Ever!

Apart from liking games I am a plant and cat mom, and I love visiting hardcore and beatdown concerts!
